## Purpose Of Sharing

When you request assistance from evo Ultimate, we may need to share relevant personal and financial information with banks, credit providers, administrators, and related service partners to help process, assess, or support your application.

## Scope

Shared information may include identification details, contact details, income and affordability information, supporting application documents, and communication needed to manage your enquiry.

## How Sharing Is Managed

We aim to share only the information reasonably required for the service you requested. We expect the parties we work with to handle shared information responsibly and in line with applicable data protection requirements.

## Consent And Enquiries

By submitting information to request our assistance, you acknowledge that relevant information may be shared for the purpose of evaluating or progressing your home loan enquiry. If you need more detail, please contact us at [lizzy.mootane@evogroup.co.za](mailto:lizzy.mootane@evogroup.co.za).
