## What We Collect

We may collect personal information that you choose to share with us, including your name, contact details, home loan enquiry details, and information needed to help assess your bond application.

## How We Use It

We use your information to:

- respond to your enquiries
- help you compare home loan options
- liaise with banks or service providers involved in your application
- improve our customer service and website experience

## When We Share Information

We only share information where it is necessary to help deliver our service, comply with legal obligations, or support a home loan application you have asked us to assist with.

## Your Rights

You may contact us to ask what personal information we hold about you, request corrections, or raise concerns about how your information is being used.
